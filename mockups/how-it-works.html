How Tokenomics Fetches Data
lock.shield
Local Only
Tokenomics reads credentials stored by each AI tool on your Mac, then calls their rate-limit APIs directly. No account is required in Tokenomics itself, and no data is sent to any server we operate. Your usage numbers never leave your machine.
Menu Bar Icon
circle.inset.filled
Outer Ring
The broader picture — a longer-horizon or secondary metric. For Claude it's the 7-day window; for Codex it's the model context window; for Gemini it's daily requests.
circle.fill
Inner Ring
Your most immediate constraint — the limit you're closest to hitting. Fills clockwise as usage increases.
smallcircle.filled.circle
Pace Dots
Show where you'd be if usage were spread evenly across the window. Dot ahead of fill means you're under pace. Only shown on time-based windows.
percent
Percentage
Your inner ring value as a number — the limit you're closest to hitting.
Usage Panel
chart.bar.fill
Usage Bars
Show how much of each rate limit window you've consumed. The bar fills from left to right as usage increases.
circle.fill
Pace Indicator
The white dot on each bar marks where even usage would be at this point in the window.
clock.arrow.circlepath
Reset Time
When the current window resets. Time-based windows show a countdown; non-time-based windows show remaining capacity instead.
Plan & Extras
person.text.rectangle
Plan Badge
Shows your plan tier for each provider. Claude, Codex, and Copilot plans are detected automatically.
dollarsign.circle
Extra Usage
Visible on Max plans with extra usage enabled. Shows how much of your monthly spending limit you've used.
Desktop Widgets
square.grid.2x2
Widget Extension
Tokenomics shares usage data with its own widget extension so you can add widgets to your desktop. No data leaves your Mac or is shared with any other app.
